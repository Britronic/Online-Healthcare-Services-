Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I schedule an appointment at Neema Hospital?

    Scheduling appointments is easy! Visit our website, register and use our online patient portal to book a convenient time slot with the desired department.

  • What insurance plans does Neema Hospital accept?

    Neema Hospital accepts a wide range of insurance plans. Please check with our billing department or visit our website for a list of accepted insurance providers and any specific requirements.

  • Are there any virtual or telemedicine services available at Neema Hospital?

    Yes, Neema Hospital offers virtual consultations and telemedicine services. You can connect with our healthcare professionals remotely for certain appointments or follow-up consultations on our online patient portal.

  • How can I access my medical records from Neema Hospital?

    Accessing your medical records is simple. You can log in to our secure patient portal. We prioritize the confidentiality and security of your health information.

  • What amenities and support services are available for patients and their families during hospital stays?

    Neema Hospital is committed to providing a comfortable experience. We offer various amenities, including waiting areas, cafeterias, and support services. Our patient care coordinators are also available to assist with any additional needs or concerns during your stay.
